Elden Ring

Why is Elden Ring a Masterpiece?

  1. Immersive and Beautiful Open World
    • The Lands Between, Elden Ring's open world, is vast and diverse, with stunning landscapes, from majestic castles to eerie swamps.
    • The game's art direction and attention to detail create a sense of wonder and immersion, making exploration a rewarding experience.
    • The world's secrets and hidden areas encourage players to delve deeper and uncover its mysteries.

  2. Challengeing and Rewarding Combat
    • Elden Ring's combat system builds upon FromSoftware's signature style, requiring patience, precision, and strategic planning.
    • The game offers a wide variety of weapons, spells, and armor, allowing players to tailor their builds and combat styles to their preferences.
    • The challenging encounters and boss fights push players to learn and improve their skills, leading to a sense of accomplishment and reward.

  3. Captive Narrative and World-Building
    • Elden Ring's narrative is cryptic and atmospheric, inviting players to piece together the story through exploration, dialogue, and lore.
    • The game's diverse cast of characters and their complex relationships add depth and intrigue to the story.
    • The world's history and mythology, hinted at through various elements, create a sense of wonder and draw players into the narrative.

  4. Freedom and Player Agency
    • The open world allows players to explore in any order they choose, fostering a sense of discovery and freedom.
    • The game's non-linear progression encourages experimentation and allows players to find their own paths through the story and challenges.
    • The wide variety of gameplay options and build paths provide players with the ability to customize their experience and find what works best for them.

  5. All Around So Much Fun!
    • I love the ability we have to customize our character and play the person we want to play.
    • I love how quest lines change the story. Ranni's Quest line is my all time favorite altercation to the story
    • The Challenge each boss fight brings provides an exhilarating and sometimes angry experience that makes you want to play more.
    • It is just so much fun.
